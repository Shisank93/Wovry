Return & Exchange Policy

15-Day Hassle-Free Returns

We want you to absolutely love your purchase

Returns

  • Items can be returned within 15 days of delivery.
  • Products must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging.
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.
  • For COD orders, refunds will be credited via bank transfer.

Exchanges

  • Size exchanges are free — we'll cover the shipping!
  • You can exchange for a different size or color of the same product.
  • Exchanges are subject to availability.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom or personalized orders.
  • Items purchased during final sale promotions.
  • Items that show signs of wear, washing, or damage.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Email us at shisankyadav3@gmail.com with your order ID.
  2. We'll send you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours.
  3. Pack the item securely and drop it off at the nearest courier partner.
  4. Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be initiated.
