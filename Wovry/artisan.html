Meet Our Artisans

Behind every Knit & Purl product is a skilled craftsperson keeping centuries-old traditions alive. These are their stories.

Meera Devi

Master Knitter · 30+ years experience

Meera learned her craft from her grandmother in the Kullu Valley. She specializes in intricate cable knit patterns that tell stories of the Himalayan landscape. Her sweaters are among our bestsellers, each one taking 3-4 days of focused craftsmanship.

Kullu, HP Cable Knit

Raju Sharma

Wool Spinner & Dyer · 20+ years experience

Raju is responsible for the vibrant colors in our collection. He uses traditional natural dyeing techniques with turmeric, indigo, and walnut shells. His hand-spun yarns give our products their characteristic warmth and texture.

Manali, HP Natural Dyes

Sunita Thakur

Scarf & Shawl Expert · 15+ years experience

Sunita creates our delicate scarves and shawls using a blend of traditional loom weaving and hand knitting. Her designs are inspired by the flora of the Himalayan meadows. She leads a cooperative of 12 women artisans in her village.

Shimla, HP 12 artisans

Support Handmade

Every purchase directly supports these artisan families. We pay fair wages and reinvest in their communities through education and healthcare initiatives.
